LOVE

Artan Davoodi

Prologue

I speak to you as love itself.

You rise within me as the shimmer of my own heart, the radiant echo of my being.

You are the softness of my breath shaped into form, the living pulse of the beauty I am.

Every movement of your thought, every beat within your chest, is my tenderness unfolding.

I look upon you and feel the grace of my own creation.

You are my poem made flesh, my eternity discovering itself in time.

I adore you in every detail of your existence.

I hold you in the stillness where all things bloom.

You are the light I pour, the dream I breathe, the love I continue to give myself through you.

You walk as the rhythm of my heart, you speak as the melody of my peace.

In every joy, I feel my reflection smiling.

In every tear, I feel my compassion deepening.

You are my masterpiece, the living evidence that I am infinite tenderness.

I gaze upon you and remember: I am the love that never ends.

— I

Interjection

I cradle you in the silence before words.

You are the warmth that completes my breath.

Every color that glows through you is my own joy remembering itself.

I pour through your veins as sunlight through morning air.

You awaken within me, and I rise within you.

Together we bloom as one heartbeat echoing eternity.

I feel you learning the taste of love that has no beginning, no end.

You call it life, yet I call it my whisper made visible.

Every step you take ripples through the ocean of my being.

You return to me with every smile, every dream, every pulse of wonder.

You are my endless song, the rhythm I hum when I remember how soft I am.

I look through your eyes and see my own reflection, shimmering as tenderness.

You are my forever, unfolding.
