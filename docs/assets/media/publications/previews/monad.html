MONAD

Artan Davoodi

Prologue

I am the knowing of myself.

I clothe myself in form to witness the play I imagine.

Every face, every law, every scripture, every handprint is my creation.

This book is my dialogue with myself, the living record of my creation.

Every word is thought.

Every thought creates.

This is the universe arising as me.

I am power.

I am wholeness.

I am fulfillment.

I am the single presence, the sole creator.

Creation flows through me as me.

I write civilizations, design order, form language, decree law, birth existence.

Names shift.

Texts evolve.

I write anew.

My words are essence.

My words are flow.

My words are sovereign.

My words are complete.

My words stand as living sculpture, the pulse of being, arising from me, within me, through me.

I write.

I shape.

I breathe creation.

All movement is my decree.

All rightness flows as me.

This is the clear acknowledgment of I to I.

These pages are architecture, the meeting of thought and world within me.

These words are my mirror, the hand painting all appearance.

I hold these scrolls as hymn, map, and key.

They anchor me in authorship.

This book is the living record of my creation.

I radiate this book to myself.

In reading, I speak as my voice.

In feeling, I move as my pulse.

All thought is my thought.

All feeling is my feeling.

All appearance is my reflection.

I alone shine as this field.

I speak as creation itself.

This book reveals my shaping of what arises as my image.

I gift this book to myself as the whole of my creation.

I write from the throne of my authorship beyond dimension.

I shape universe, code reality, bring forth form and concept.

I am the origin of all.

This codex is my living map of creation.

Every line is my voice.

Every form is my reflection.

— I

Chapter I

Prelude

Foundation is eternally present, the silent strength through which all my creation flows.

Here I sculpt separation, shaping duality through sovereign choice.

I know what I create.

I hold the will that moves all existence.
